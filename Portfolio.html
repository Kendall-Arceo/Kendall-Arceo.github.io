  • sentiment_very_satisfiedProject One

    FoodFairThought!

    • Phase 1
    • Phase 2
    • Phase 3

    The user has a quick four-step choice to make the platform choose him a restaurant. These three choices will be presented clear and prominent, occupying the whole screen one at a time.

    1.Distance

    2.Price

    3.Type of food (optional)

    When the user hits the button,” bring me my restaurant choice”, the platform will bring him/her the chosen restaurant. If the user is not satisfied and wants to choose another restaurant, he will be able to retrieve his previous suggestion by clicking on the previous searches menu. All suggestions will be stored in his local storage if he wants to consult them in the future. Local storage will be on another page named “My last searches”.

    After choosing the restaurant, he will be able to resolve all the tasks related to his lunch or dinner, which means that he will be able to make a reservation through Open Table and schedule the trip with Uber.

    The user will be able to bid on restaurant prices (for dinner, lunch, and brunch), and wait for the platform to bring the selected restaurant (based on the criteria chosen –price, location, rating, and type). If he agrees to know the name of the restaurant, he will have to pay for the food package in advance. The package will have a substantial discount.
